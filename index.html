About Our Church

The Nigerian Seventh-day Adventist Church of Atlanta is a vibrant, 100+ member, English-speaking church of multi-cultural heritage of Nigeria, living in the Atlanta metropolitan area.

We are an enthusiastic soul-winning Church preparing a people to meet their God. Our passion is to finish God's work in our lifetime.

If you are looking for a Church home where there is deep commitment and dedication in worshiping God in truth and in sincerity, then look no further. Our congregation represents the rich cultural diversity of Nigeria, while being united in our love for Christ and our community.

We believe in making a difference in our local community through outreach programs, health ministries, and spiritual development initiatives. Join us as we grow together in faith and service.

Pastor Olufemi (Femi) Okorie

Lead Pastor

Our Ministries

There are lots of ways that we touch our community and many more opportunities exist to make a difference.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to develop a personal relationship with Christ through Bible study, social activities, and service projects.

Learn More

Music Ministry

Enhancing worship through choirs, instrumental groups, and praise teams that blend traditional Nigerian music with contemporary Christian worship.

Learn More

Food Pantry

Providing nutritious food to families in need in our community, working to combat hunger in the Atlanta metropolitan area.

Learn More

Health Ministry

Promoting holistic health through education, screenings, and wellness programs that address physical, mental, and spiritual wellbeing.

Learn More

Prayer Ministry

Standing in the gap in prayer for individuals, families, our church, community, and world events through intercessory prayer groups.
